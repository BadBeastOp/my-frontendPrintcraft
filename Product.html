Premium Matte Business Cards

Our matte finish business cards are printed on ultra-thick 16pt card stock, giving them a premium weight and feel that makes a strong first impression. The silk matte coating creates a sophisticated, non-reflective surface that's smooth to the touch.

Every card is printed using state-of-the-art HP Indigo digital presses, ensuring precise color reproduction and razor-sharp text — even at very small font sizes. Our automated quality inspection catches any imperfections before your cards are packaged.

  • 16pt extra-thick card stock — premium weight feel
  • Silk matte coating — smooth, non-reflective finish
  • Full bleed printing — vibrant, edge-to-edge color
  • HP Indigo digital press — razor sharp text and images
  • Die-cut to exact size — clean, professional edges
  • Eco-friendly soy-based inks — better for the planet
